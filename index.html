How to Play

Instructions will disappear when difficulty is chosen

  1. Choose a difficulty (easy will choose 3 random characters, and you will have 2 enemies. Medium will have 4 random characters, and you will have 3 enemies. Hard will have 5 characters, and you will have 4 enemies.
  2. Choose an enemy to fight from the enemies section. Choose carefully, only select paths will lead to victory.
  3. Once an enemy is chosen, the enemy will set up in the defender location. Click attack to begin the fight.
  4. Once the battleground is loaded, attack the enemy until the enemy runs out of HP. Defeat all enemies to win!
